I was tasked with creating the visual identity for the annual Tech Innovators Conference. This included designing the event logo, posters, social media graphics, and attendee badges. The design aimed to reflect the cutting-edge nature of the technology industry while maintaining a professional and engaging look, ensuring a strong visual presence across all event materials.
